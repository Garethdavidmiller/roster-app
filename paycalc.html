📈 Pay Rise Back Pay

Use when a pay award has been backdated to April
▼

When a pay award is backdated, all the extra money owed is paid as a single lump sum. Select the first period covered by the new rate, enter the old and new values, then choose which payslip the lump sum lands on.

Chiltern's pay anniversary is 1 April — pre-set to the April period of your current tax year. Change only if the award started at a different point.
